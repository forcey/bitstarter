I love buying eBooks. There are many options though; and these options come with pros and cons. Kindle books look beautiful in all the reading devices, and the progress syncs seamfully, but you have to buy the books from Amazon and you can't get complex formattings. PDFs on the other hand, are absolutely beautiful to read on computers and big tablets. However when you hop on the train in the morning, it's really painful to find where you left off on your computer last night.

I'm planning to develop a PDF reader that syncs progress across your devices. There will be an iPad version, a Mac version and a PC version. Simply log in with the same account, and the PDFs you opened from another device will automatically appear. Open it and you are right where you left off.
